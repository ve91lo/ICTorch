# Application Examples in Technology

## Autonomous Driving Technology

In the logistics industry, autonomous trucks reduce transportation costs and improve efficiency, especially in long-haul transport.

### Example 1
Tesla's autonomous driving technology provides self-driving solutions for commercial vehicles, enhancing logistics efficiency and safety.

### Example 2
JD Logistics in China is testing autonomous delivery vehicles to complete "last-mile" deliveries in urban areas.

**Additional Notes:** Autonomous driving technology is also applied in public transportation (e.g., self-driving buses) and personal mobility (e.g., autonomous cars).

----

## Quantum Computing

### Application Example
Quantum computing is used in drug discovery for complex molecular modeling, accelerating the development of new medicines.

### Example 1
IBM's quantum computing platform, Qiskit, is used to simulate chemical reactions, helping researchers develop more efficient drugs.

### Example 2
Google's quantum computing research team leverages quantum computers to optimize logistics networks, solving modern supply chain issues.

**Additional Notes:** Quantum computing is also applied in cryptography (e.g., breaking complex codes) and weather forecasting (e.g., simulating climate change).
