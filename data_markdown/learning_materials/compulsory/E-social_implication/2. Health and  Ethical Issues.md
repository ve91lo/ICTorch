# Issue
The rapid development of technology may negatively impact the environment, such as increasing electronic waste and energy consumption.

# Solutions
- Use eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient designs to reduce environmental impact.
- Promote recycling and reuse of electronic products.

## Example
Companies can design modular smartphones that allow users to replace individual components, extending product life and reducing electronic waste.

# Additional Notes
Sustainable development is not just a corporate social responsibility but also enhances brand image and competitiveness.
