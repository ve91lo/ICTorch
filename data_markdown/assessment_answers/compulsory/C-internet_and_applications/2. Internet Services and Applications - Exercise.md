# Potential Challenges

## Digital Divide
Unequal access to technology and the internet can exacerbate existing social inequalities, leaving some populations behind in terms of opportunities and resources.

## Privacy and Security
The proliferation of digital technologies raises concerns about data privacy and security, as personal information and sensitive data can be vulnerable to breaches and misuse.

## Dependence on Technology
Increasing reliance on technology can lead to issues such as decreased face-to-face interactions, over-reliance on digital tools, and potential impacts on mental health and social skills.
