# Explanation

- **Column ~P**: This is the negation of P.
- **Column ~Q**: This is the negation of Q.
- **Column P v ~Q**: This is the disjunction (OR) of P and ~Q.
- **Column ~(P v ~Q)**: This is the negation of the previous column.
- **Column R v ~P**: This is the disjunction (OR) of R and ~P.
- **Column ~(P v ~Q) ^ (R v ~P)**: This is the conjunction (AND) of the two previous columns. The output is 1 only when both columns are true.
